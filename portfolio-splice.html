splice

mobile app

Splice is a real-time multi user bill splitting mobile app.

Splice was built to ease the process of bill splitting. Simply by taking a photo of your receipt, you, as a host, are then able to invite your friends to the receipt where each person is able to select the items that they ordered. Once selected, each person's tax and tip is calculated based on their respective subtotal.

Each person is then directed to Venmo to send the appropriate amount to the host. Once everyone has paid the host for their portion, the receipt is closed.

08/2019

Tech Stack: React Native, Expo, Cloud Firestore, Express, Taggun.io, Native Base
